## The 4 Pramanas

| Pramana | Meaning | Example |
|---------|---------|---------|
| Pratyaksha | Direct Observation | "I measured pH. It was 6.8." |
| Anumana | Logical Inference | "Leaves yellow, needs water." |
| Upamana | Comparison | "Works like a car engine." |
| Shabda | Testimony | "Scientists say..." |

---

## 5-Step Verification

1. Pratijna — What is claimed?
2. Hetu — How is it known?
3. Udaharana — How to verify?
4. Upanaya — What to do?
5. Nigamana — Final verdict

---

## 9 Hallucination Flags

Anonymous authority | Citation without reference | Bare assertions | Absolute claims | Hype language | Impossible timeframes | Zero-downside | Total-solution | Emotional manipulation
